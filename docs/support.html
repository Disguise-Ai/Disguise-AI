Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get better response suggestions?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and fill out more information about your texting style. The more we know about how you text, the better we can match your vibe.

How do I use the screenshot feature?

Tap the camera icon in the chat, select a screenshot of your conversation, and we'll analyze it to give you response options.

Is my data private?

Yes. We take privacy seriously. Your conversations and screenshots are processed securely and we don't share your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at support@dontpanic.digital and we'll delete your account and all associated data.

The app isn't connecting. What do I do?

Make sure you have an internet connection. If the problem persists, try closing and reopening the app, or contact support.
